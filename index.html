School History


Founded in 1909, Lakeshore High School has grown from a small local institution into a vibrant center of learning and community. Nestled along the scenic shores of Washington Dc, the school was established with the mission of providing high-quality education while nurturing curiosity, creativity, and character in every student. Over the years, Lakeshore High has expanded its academic programs, extracurricular activities, and state-of-the-art facilities, becoming known for excellence in Sciences, Mathematics, Humanities, Languages, and Sports. The school prides itself on fostering a supportive environment where students are encouraged to explore their passions, develop critical thinking skills, and contribute positively to society. Today, Lakeshore High School continues to uphold its tradition of academic excellence and community engagement, preparing students to thrive in an ever-changing world.
